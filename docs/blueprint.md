# **App Name**: LogiSim WMS

## Core Features:

- Gestion des Tiers: Permet l'ajout, la consultation et la modification des clients, fournisseurs et transporteurs.
- Flux Entrant (Réception): Gère la création et la réception des Bons de Commande (BC), mettant à jour le stock et l'historique.
- Flux Sortant (Expédition): Permet la création de Bons de Livraison (BL), le contrôle de stock, la préparation de commandes (picking), et la génération de la Lettre de Voiture (CMR).
- Gestion des Stocks et Inventaire: Offre la consultation du stock, l'historique des mouvements, et la possibilité d'ajuster l'inventaire.
- Picking Route Optimization: Using AI, optimizes the picking route in the warehouse to minimize travel distance.
- Document Generation Tool: The system can use its understanding of warehouse processes to incorporate the ideal verbiage, information, and layout into the various documents that get generated. For example, a tool for writing explanatory text into documents.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#3F51B5) for a professional and reliable feel, representing logistics and trust.
- Background color: Very light grey (#F5F5F5) to ensure readability and a clean interface.
- Accent color: Light blue (#64B5F6), an analogous color to the primary, used for interactive elements and highlights, offering good contrast while maintaining a cohesive theme.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif with a modern, neutral look.
- Use clear and simple icons representing each function (truck for transport, boxes for stock, etc.).
- Maintain a clear, structured, menu-driven interface with distinct sections for each module.
- Subtle transitions between menus and actions to enhance user experience without being distracting.
